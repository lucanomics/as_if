# Content Policy

- Focus all content on practical English communication drills.
- Do not present legal, immigration, medical, or mental health guidance as advice.
- Clearly separate language practice examples from real-world professional guidance.
- Keep scenarios realistic, respectful, and non-sensational.
- Phrase examples must not be used to fabricate facts, evade official processes, or manipulate outcomes.
- Safer alternatives are language-practice examples only, not legal, medical, or immigration advice.
